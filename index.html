CHAPTER II

The Process

Every athlete is different. Every goal is different. Every journey is different. That's why I don't believe in universal methods. I believe in understanding the individual first.

01

Assess

Every journey starts with understanding the athlete: physique, lifestyle, training history, nutrition habits, recovery, mindset, strengths and weaknesses.

02

Build

We build the foundation: training, nutrition, recovery and execution. Every variable has a purpose. Nothing is random.

03

Refine

Progress creates information. Information creates better decisions. Coaching is adjusting every detail until everything works together.

04

Evolve

There is no finish line. Every version of yourself becomes the starting point for the next one.

CHAPTER III

The Builder

I never set out to become a coach. I became obsessed with understanding bodybuilding.

My journey began with rehabilitation. A knee injury brought me into a gym. Curiosity kept me there. Over time, bodybuilding became more than training. It became the way I approached discipline, learning and personal growth.

For more than twenty-five years I have dedicated my life to understanding bodybuilding, not only as a discipline to practice, but as a system to study, refine and teach.

IAM was not created to build a following. It was created to build a standard.

Bodybuilder

02

Coach

03

Student of the sport

Never stop learning. Never stop refining. Never stop building.
